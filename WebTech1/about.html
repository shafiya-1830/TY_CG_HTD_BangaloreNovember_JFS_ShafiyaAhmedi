About us

Wildlife sanctuary has evolved from the preservation of endangered species in the early 1900’s to maintain quality and beauty of wildlife.
The purpose of this system is for smooth administration and to get a brief idea of overall expenses and profit the sanctuary deals with. The system keeps a backup of the tourist data which includes their ticket detail, the fare they pay and the donations.
The main purpose of this management system is to maintain healthy populations of native animals in balance with their environment.
